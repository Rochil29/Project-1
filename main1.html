ABOUT

US

New Horizon College of Engineering is an Autonomous college affiliated to Visvesvaraya Technological University(VTU), approved by the All India Council for Technical Education(AICTE) & University Grants Commission(UGC). It is accredited by NAAC with an 'A'​ grade & the National Board of Accreditation(NBA). It is an ISO 9001:2008 certified Institution. New Horizon College of Engineering is located in the heart of the IT capital of India, Bangalore.
